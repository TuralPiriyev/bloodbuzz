Every run balances speed against exposure. Master the systems or end up on the windowsill.

System / Movement

Dual Movement

Walk to stay silent and hidden in the grass, or burn stamina for a fast, buzzing flight burst that closes distance in an instant — and draws every eye in the yard.

He slaps high, stomps low, and adapts to where your swarm keeps landing. Get predictable and he'll start guessing right — and one hit ends your run.
